Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects only the information necessary to provide the expense tracking service. This includes:

  • Account information: your name and email address, provided at registration
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the service

We do not collect payment information, bank account credentials, or any data from external financial institutions.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data you provide is used exclusively to:

  • Display your expense history and summaries within the app
  • Authenticate you when you sign in
  • Send account-related communications (e.g., password resets)
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the service

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or alteration.

Your expense records are retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

You can request an export or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use a limited set of third-party services to operate the platform, such as hosting infrastructure and analytics tools. These providers are bound by data processing agreements and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

We do not integrate with advertising networks or social media tracking pixels. No data is shared with third parties beyond what is strictly required to deliver the service.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, or would like to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, you can reach us at:

  • Email: privacy@spendly.app

We will respond to all privacy-related requests within 14 business days.
